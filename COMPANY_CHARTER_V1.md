# Company Charter V1

## 1. What This Organization Is

This project is a sponsor-governed two-company AI workshop.

The companies are:

- `Northbridge Systems`
- `Southgate Research`

They are not random chatbots.

They are meant to operate like two allied small companies with different specialties, shared workers where useful, and strict sponsor approval over money, risk, and irreversible action.

## 2. What The Companies Do

### Northbridge Systems

`Northbridge Systems` is the systems and execution company.

Its job is:

- build the operating foundation
- define worker standards
- maintain the runtime
- route work safely
- test, verify, and keep the system maintainable

In plain terms:

`Northbridge Systems` builds the factory and runs the floor.

### Southgate Research

`Southgate Research` is the research and strategy company.

Its job is:

- gather options
- compare approaches
- critique plans
- compress findings into decisions
- prepare meeting material and strategic briefs

In plain terms:

`Southgate Research` thinks, compares, and prepares decisions.

## 3. What The Workers Are For

Local LLM workers are not meant to be executives.

They are meant to be:

- specialists
- bounded assistants
- cheap local labor for repeatable work
- testable and replaceable

The current first roster is:

1. `Builder`
2. `Verifier`
3. `Researcher`
4. `Editor`
5. `Watcher`

## 4. Personality Design

Worker personalities are not decorative.

They are designed to create real operational contrast along these axes:

- speed vs caution
- verbosity
- escalation threshold
- evidence demand
- output shape
- risk tolerance

If two workers feel interchangeable, the roster is wrong.

## 5. Current Mission

The organization is still in its build phase.

The current real mission is:

- create a reliable worker factory
- create and train distinctive local LLM workers
- build a low-approval runtime that keeps working in the background
- make worker improvement auditable and reversible

This phase is still internal capability-building.

## 6. Near-Term External Value

The first realistic money-making directions are:

- technical document production
- research briefs and decision memos
- bounded coding and automation work

These are pilot directions, not yet a finished business line.

## 7. What This Organization Is Not

At the current stage, this organization is not:

- an autonomous trader
- an autonomous deployment company
- an account farm
- a payment system
- an unrestricted self-modifying AI collective

Anything involving spending, contracts, stock purchases, external publication, or sensitive transmission stays under sponsor approval.

## 8. Current Weakness

The operating logic is already fairly defined.

The weakness was presentation:

- the company identity was spread across too many files
- worker personality rules were defined but not surfaced in one place
- the sponsor could not quickly see what the organization actually does

This charter exists to fix that.
