## 3. Current Recommended Position

Recommended answer:

- choose the public summary as the first actually released public asset
- keep the profile as the second release candidate
- keep outreach copy ready but gated as a supporting outbound asset

Reason:

- the summary is the shortest and cleanest standalone asset
- it communicates the product without requiring platform-specific tuning first
- it is the least likely place for claim drift or hidden implementation language
