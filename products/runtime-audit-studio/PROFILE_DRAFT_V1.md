## 2. Headline Option

Evidence-first runtime audits for local LLM systems that look strong in demos but feel unreliable in real operation

## 3. Short Profile Version

I review local LLM and AI workflow runtimes and turn messy operational signals into a bounded audit someone can actually act on.

The work is direct: read the evidence, say what is healthy, say what is weak, and recommend the next fix without pretending the system is more mature than it is.

## 4. Medium Profile Version

`Runtime Audit Studio` is for teams that already have a local AI system running and need a sharper answer than "it seems okay."

This is a good fit when the real question is operational:

- is unattended execution actually healthy
- is the local runtime stable enough to trust
- what is the current bottleneck
- what should be fixed before stronger production claims are made

My style is evidence-first and bounded. I do not hide implementation work inside an audit quote, and I do not sell autonomous AI mythology as if it were system reliability.

The goal is a reviewable operating readout, not vague optimism and not a pile of low-level logs with no decision structure.
