## 3. Current Recommended Position

Recommended answer:

- choose the service page as the first released public asset
- keep summary as the next likely release candidate
- keep profile and post ready but gated

Reason:

- the service page is the clearest standalone first-touch asset
- it explains the offer without overexpanding claims
- the other three are stronger as supporting or follow-on assets
